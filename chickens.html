Meet Our Flock!

We raise a variety of chicken breeds on our farm, each with their own unique characteristics and personalities.

Our Chicken Breeds

  • Rhode Island Reds

    Rhode Island Reds are a popular breed known for their rich, brown eggs and friendly personalities.

  • Barred Plymouth Rock

    Barred Plymouth Rock chickens are a hardy and friendly breed, known for their distinctive barred feathers and excellent egg-laying abilities.
